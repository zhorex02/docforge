## FAQ

**Q: What information do you need?**
A: Company name, address, tax ID (optional), client details, and a list of items with quantities and prices. I'll handle the formatting.

**Q: Can you match my brand colors?**
A: Yes! Send me your brand colors (hex codes) and logo, and I'll customize the design. Available in Standard and Premium.

**Q: Do you do recurring invoices?**
A: I can create a template you can reuse. For automated recurring invoices, check my Premium tier.

**Q: What formats do you deliver?**
A: PDF (standard). I can also provide the HTML source if you need to make future edits.

**Q: Can you do other languages besides English and Spanish?**
A: Currently English and Spanish. Other languages available on request in Premium tier.

## My Workflow Per Order (internal)

1. Client sends requirements via Fiverr message (5 min to read)
2. I format data as JSON (5 min)
3. Run: `engine.generate("invoice", data)` (30 seconds)
4. Review PDF visually (2 min)
5. Deliver via Fiverr (1 min)
6. **Total my time: ~15 minutes per order**
7. **Effective rate at Basic $50: ~$200/hour**
